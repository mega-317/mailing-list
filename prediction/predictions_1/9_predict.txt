### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- **Event Name:** IEEE Artificial Intelligence x Software Engineering (aka AIxSoftware)
- **Location and Date:** October 1-3, 2025, The Hills Hotel, Laguna Hills, California
- **Format:** Hybrid
- **Co-located Events:** Co-located with AIxSET, AIxHEART, AIxB
- **Purpose:** AIxSE is an international forum for academia and industries to exchange visions and ideas in the state of the art and practice, as well as to identify the emerging topics and define the synergy between the research in AI and Software Engineering (SE).

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- **[AIxSE] Authors are invited to submit an 8-page (regular), 4-page (short), and 2-page (poster and demo) manuscript in double-column IEEE format.** 
- **[AIxSE] Manuscripts must be written in English and follow the instructions in the Manuscript Formatting and Templates page.**
- **[AIxSE] All paper submissions must be submitted electronically through EasyChair. Only PDF format is accepted.**
- **[AIxSE] The authors of accepted papers must guarantee their paper will be presented at the conference. Original material is required where copyright of all parts is owned by the authors.**
