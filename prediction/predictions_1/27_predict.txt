### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- Excited to announce the **17th Symposium on Search-Based Software Engineering (SSBSE 2025)**, taking place on **16 November 2025** in Seoul!  
- Co-located with **ASE**.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- **[SSBSE 2025] Submission Deadline: August 21, 2025**
- **[SSBSE 2025] Notification: September 25, 2025**
- **[SSBSE 2025] Camera-Ready: October 3, 2025**

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- **[SSBSE 2025] We invite high-quality research submissions that push the boundaries of SBSE, with a special focus this year on the transformative role of AI and optimization in software engineering.**
- **[SSBSE 2025] Key topics include:**
  - Novel applications of SBSE, especially in emerging domains
  - Theoretical advances in search algorithms for software engineering
  - Rigorous empirical evaluations with reproducible results
  - Industrial experiences and real-world deployments
  - Integrations of SBSE with AI, machine learning, and cutting-edge technologies
- **[SSBSE 2025] Accepted papers will be considered for a special journal issue in the Automated Software Engineering journal.**
