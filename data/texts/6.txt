The Department of Informatics Engineering at the University of Coimbra (Portugal) is pleased to announce an international call for applications for one tenure-track position at the level of Assistant Professor in the field of Informatics Engineering, with a focus on the sub-areas of Dependability or Software Engineering. 

Applications are open until August 7, 2025.
More details and the official application portal can be found here: https://apply.uc.pt/IT136-25-15300

Please feel free to share this opportunity with strong candidates who may be interested in a permanent position at a leading European university and looking to advance their careers in an excellent academic environment.
