ACM/IEEE International Symposium on Code Generation and Optimization (CGO 2026)

Call for Papers - First Submission Deadline

The Call for Papers<https://2026.cgo.org/track/cgo-2026-papers> is out for the next International Symposium on Code Generation and Optimization (CGO’26)<https://2026.cgo.org/>, taking place next year in Sydney, Australia.

CGO provides a premier venue to bring together researchers and practitioners working at the interface of hardware and software on a wide range of optimization and code generation techniques and related issues. The conference spans the spectrum from purely static to fully dynamic approaches, and from pure software-based methods to specific architectural features and support for code generation and optimisation.

The first submission round is coming up, with the following deadlines:

  *   Paper Submission: 29 May 2025
  *   Author Rebuttal Period: 8–10 July 2025
  *   Paper Notification: 21 July 2025

For more information, please visit the CGO’26 website<https://2026.cgo.org/>.

We look forward to receiving your submissions!
