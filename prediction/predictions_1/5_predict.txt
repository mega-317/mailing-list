### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- [SANER 2026] Research Track: Submissions must be original, not published, accepted, or under review elsewhere and should not exceed 12 pages (with the last 2 pages reserved for references only).
- [SANER 2026] Industrial Papers Track: Submissions should not exceed 12 pages (with the last 2 pages reserved for references only) for full papers; talk proposals should not exceed 2 pages.
- [SANER 2026] Short Papers and Posters Track: Submissions should not exceed 6 pages (including all text, figures, references, and appendices); posters should not exceed 2 pages.
- [SANER 2026] Early Research Achievement (ERA) Track: Submissions should not exceed 6 pages (with the last 1 page reserved for references only).
- [SANER 2026] Reproducibility Studies and Negative Results (RENE) Track: Submissions should not exceed 5 pages for appendices to conference submissions or previous work and should not exceed 12 pages for new reproducibility studies and new descriptions of negative results (with the last 2 pages reserved for references only).
- [SANER 2026] Tool Demo Track: Submissions should not exceed 5 pages.
- [SANER 2026] Journal-First Papers Track: Submissions should not exceed 1 page (for the main submission).
- [SANER 2026] Registered Report Track: Submissions should not exceed 7 pages (with the last 1 page reserved for references only).
