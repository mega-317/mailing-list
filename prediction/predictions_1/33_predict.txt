### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Regular Papers: max 10+2 pages - Novel research contributions with substantive evaluation.
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Vision / New Ideas Papers: max 6+1 pages - Innovative and forward-looking ideas which may not yet be fully developed or evaluated.
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Tool Demonstration Papers: max 4+1 pages - Practical insights and tools; include appendix with demo outline.
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Exemplar Papers: max 6+1 pages - Engineering-focused implementations or specs of DTs in certain domains.
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Formatting: IEEE Proceedings Template (LaTeX or Word) must be used.
- **[EDTconf 2025]** Page limits are strict. Papers must be original and not under review elsewhere. ORCID usage is recommended.
