=========== Workshops at ER 2025 ===========

https://er2025.ensma.fr/workshops/

Continuing a long tradition, ER 2025 will host workshops to be held in
conjunction with the main conference. These workshops serve as an intensive
collaborative forum for exchanging innovative ideas about conceptual
modeling, and for discovering new frontiers for its use.

Details regarding the Call for Papers for each workshop can be found on the
respective workshop's website (linked below):

*Fundamentals of Conceptual Modeling (FCM)*
https://ae-ainf.aau.at/fcm/


*6th International Workshop on Conceptual Modeling for Life Sciences (CMLS)*
https://purl.org/cmls-2025


*3rd International Workshop Modeling in the Age of Large Language Models
(LLM4Modeling)*
https://www.unifr.ch/inf/digits/en/events/3rd-international-workshop-modeling-in-the-age-of-large-language-models-(llm4modeling).html


*11th International Workshop on Ontologies and Conceptual Modeling
(OntoCom)*https://ontocomworkshops.github.io/OntoCom2025/


*6th International Workshop on Quality and Measurement of Model-Driven
Software Development (QUAMES)*https://quames.testar.org


*Important Dates*================================
Common deadlines in all the five workshops are:
Submission Deadline: *27 June 2025*
Notification:* 28 July 2025*
