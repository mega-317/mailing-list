### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- **Event Name**: ISSRE 2025 (International Symposium on Software Reliability Engineering)
- **Location**: SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil
- **Event Dates**: October 21-24, 2025
- **Event Focus**: ISSRE is the leading conference on software reliability, focusing on approaches, techniques, and tools for assessing, predicting, and improving the reliability, safety, and security of software systems.

- **Workshops at ISSRE 2025**: Workshops provide additional opportunities for collaboration and information exchange for both practitioners from industry and academic researchers. They aim to discuss recent developments and open challenges in engineering high-assurance software and systems, allowing the exchange of ideas at an early stage before maturation.
