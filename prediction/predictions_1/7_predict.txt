### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- The IEEE International Conference on Software Maintenance and Evolution (ICSME) is one of the premier academic conferences for researchers and practitioners working on the maintenance and evolution of software systems.
- ICSME 2025 will be held in Auckland, New Zealand.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- [ICSME 2025] Conference Dates: September 7 to September 12, 2025.
- [ICSME 2025] Early-bird registration deadline: August 1, 2025 (AoE).
- [ICSME 2025] Application and Reference Letters for Student Volunteers need to be submitted by July 15, 2025, at 23:59 AoE.

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- [ICSME 2025] To register, visit Attending > Registration on the website.
- [Student Volunteers] Get involved, meet mentors, and experience the conference from the inside. Application is still open!
