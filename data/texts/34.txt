Dear all,

We are looking for a (up to three-years contract) Post-Doc for the
Johannes Kepler University (JKU) Linz Institute of Technology
(LIT).

The Post-Doc is expected to conduct software engineering research in the
JKU / Dynatrace Austria Co-Innovation Lab, with a
particular focus on one or several of the following topics:

(o) Distributed Software Systems and Software Architectures, esp.
Distributed Stream Processing Systems
(o) Cloud Computing and Software Performance, esp. in Large, Distributed
Data Systems
(o) Real-Time, Scalable Data Processing and Querying

Job duties:

(o) Conduct Software Engineering research in the fields mentioned above
(o) Lay the foundation for the next generation of highly scalable,
data-driven software applications in collaboration with one of
the industry leaders in software intelligence, i.e., Dynatrace
(https://research.dynatrace.com/)
(o) Apply for research grants
(o) Publish, present and disseminate research findings in journals,
conferences and workshops
(o) Collaborate with fellow JKU researchers and academic and industrial
partners in Austria and abroad
(o) (Co-)supervise Ph.D./Master’s/Bachelor’s students

Your qualifications:

(o) Strong software engineering background and with experience in one or
multiple of the following fields: Distributed Software
Systems and Software Architectures, especially Distributed Stream
Processing Systems, Real-Time, Scalable Data Processing and
Querying, Cloud Computing, Software Performance, especially in Large,
Distributed Data Systems and Parallel Computing
(o) The successful candidate must hold a Doctorate/Ph.D. degree in
Computer Science or a related field
(o) Experience and/or expertise in a minimum of one of the corresponding
research areas as mentioned above
(o) Experience in writing academic/scientific publications and
presenting scientific work at international events
(o) Hands-on experience in object-oriented programming
(o) Experiences with Apache Spark, Apache Flink, Apache Kafka and
Kubernetes are desired
(o) Strong proficiency (C1 level) in English (both oral and written)

Apply now via the link below (Deadline Aug 10) or contact me in case you
have any questions. Details can be found here:
