### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- The Faculty of Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Victoria is looking to fill up to 4 research stream assistant professor positions at the intersection of Software Engineering and AI.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- [Assistant Professors Position] Application Deadline: Apply by August 15, 2025.
- [Assistant Professors Position] Start Date: Positions are expected to commence January 1, 2026, or soon thereafter.

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- [Assistant Professors Position] Qualified candidates must have a Ph.D. in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical or Computer Engineering (with a research focus in Software), or an equivalent field or be within a year of completion.
- [Assistant Professors Position] Applicants must have a commitment to teaching software engineering courses at the senior undergraduate level (3rd and 4th year) as part of our Bachelor of Software Engineering Program, and graduate software engineering courses.
- [Assistant Professors Position] Applicants must provide a plan for graduate student supervision, with prior experience in supervising or mentoring students considered an asset.
- [Assistant Professors Position] Given the impact of Generative AI on Software Engineering, we encourage applicants that are engaged with these topics as part of their existing or future research program.
