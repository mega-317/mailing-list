### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- **Event Name**: ACM / IEEE 28th International Conference on Model Driven Engineering Languages and Systems (MODELS)
- **Event Description**: MODELS 2025 will host a number of workshops during the three days before the main conference. The workshops will provide a collaborative forum for a group of typically 15 to 30 participants to exchange recent and/or preliminary results, conduct intensive discussions on a particular topic, or coordinate efforts between representatives of a technical community.
