Dear colleagues,

Applications are now open for the 2025 Summer School on Informatics 
Education Research (SCHIER).

||SCHIER: the only summer school in Europe exclusively focused on 
research in Informatics Education*||

[When] 8–12 September 2025
[Where] UCLouvain, Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium
[Who should attend] PhD students, postdocs, and researchers with an 
interest in Informatics Education Research

Building on the success of its inaugural edition last year, SCHIER 2025 
offers a week of:
- Expert-led lectures and interactive sessions on key topics and 
methodologies.
- Collaborative discussions on emerging research challenges.
- Networking opportunities that foster meaningful, long-term 
collaboration and advancement.

||Applications are now open, limited to 40 participants on a first-come, 
first-served basis.||

[Basic registration fees]
- EUR 350 for Participants from IE member institutes >> Join us: 
https://www.informatics-europe.org/join-us/current-members.html
- EUR 400 for other participants

[Application Deadline] 25 June 2025

Learn more and apply now: 
https://www.informatics-europe.org/education/informatics-education-research-school.html
Share flyers with your students and colleagues: 
https://www.informatics-europe.org/images/SCHIER/2025/SCHIER2025-flyer.pdf

Don’t miss this unique opportunity to engage with a vibrant community of 
researchers shaping the future of Informatics Education in Europe!

Best regards,
Informatics Europe

Remark *: Informatics Education is a growing and vital field recognised 
by the EU Council.
