### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- A special issue on Agentic AI in Software is planned in *ACM Transactions on Software Engineering and Methodology* (TOSEM) in 2026, and papers are being solicited for this purpose.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Paper submission deadline: November 1, 2025
- [TOSEM Special Issue] First reviews appear: January 31, 2025
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Revisions are due: March 31, 2026
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Outcome of the revised papers: May 2026
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Papers appear online in ACM Digital Library: July 2026
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Special issue on agentic AI in TOSEM is planned: August 2026

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Authors can mention one of the following categories in the cover letter of the paper:
  - [Auto] Automation of SE tasks via agents 
  - [Trust] Enhancing trust in agent outputs 
  - [Integrate] Integration of agents into existing software workflows 
  - [A-H] Agent – human interaction in SE teams and team dynamics 
  - [Oth] Any other agent in SE topics not covered in the preceding list.
- [TOSEM Special Issue] Submit papers by selecting the radio button “Special issue 2025: Agentic AI in Software” in manuscriptcentral: https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/tosem
