* The International Symposium on*

*Sustainable Systems Technologies and Green Computing (STGC)*



*1**5**-17 October 2025 | Dubrovnik, Croatia*

*https://flta-conference.org/flta-2025/sts.php
<https://flta-conference.org/flta-2025/sts.php>*



*A **Hyb**rid Conference*



Sponsored by IEEE Croatia Section







We are pleased to invite you to submit your original research papers, case
studies, and industry insights to our upcoming symposium. STGC is
co-located with the 3rd International Conference on Federated Learning
Technologies and Applications (FLTA 2025), which will take place on October
14-17, 2025, in Dubrovnik, Croatia.

STS aims to address the growing demand for sustainable, energy-efficient,
and resilient software systems by exploring innovative approaches in
technology and system development. We welcome contributions on topics
including, but not limited to:



   - Energy-Efficient Software Engineering
   - Sustainable Architectures
   - AI for Sustainability
   - IoT-Enabled Sustainability
   - Green Computing
   - Scalability and Adaptability
   - Blockchain for Sustainability
   - Technology , Sociology, and Economy for Sustainable Development
   - Software Engineering for Sustainability.



Accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore in the proceedings of FLTA
2025.



*Key Dates:*

   - *Submission Deadline:* August 2, 2025
   - *Symposium Date:* October 15, 2025
   - *Location:* Dubrovnik, Croatia



For more details on submission guidelines and the symposium's scope, please
visit our official webpage: STGC at FLTA 2025
<https://flta-conference.org/flta-2025/sts.php>

Should you have any questions or require further information, please feel
free to contact me directly at [log in to unmask]



We look forward to your valuable contributions and hope to see you in
Dubrovnik!
