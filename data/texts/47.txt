1st International Summer School on LLM-based Agents for Software Engineering (LLMA4SE 2025)
September 1-3, 2025 - University of Extremadura, Cáceres, Spain
https://i3lab.unex.es/summer-school/
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The University of Extremadura is pleased to announce the 1st International Summer School on LLM-based Agents for Software Engineering (LLMA4SE 2025).

This summer school targets researchers, PhD students, and professionals interested in the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Agentic AI into the field of software engineering.

Topics include:
* LLM-based agents for software development tasks (e.g., requirements analysis, coding, testing, deployment).
* Evaluation and benchmarking of agent-based systems.
* Human-agent collaboration in software engineering.

The program includes a mix of lectures and hands-on workshops, delivered by internationally recognized experts in AI and software engineering.

Confirmed speakers include:
* Davide Di Ruscio, Università degli Studi dell'Aquila, Italy.
* Jie Zhang, King's College London, UK
* Antonio García Domínguez, University of York, UK
* Jesús M. González Barahona, Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, Spain
* Massimo Tisi, IMT Atlantique, Nantes, France
* Merve Astekin, SINTEF Digital, Norway
* Adela Nedisan Videsjorden, SINTEF Digital, Norway
* Simeon Tverdal, SINTEF Digital, Norway
* Olivier Perard, Oracle EMEA, Spain

Registration details:
* Free of charge.
* Includes all course materials, coffee breaks, daily lunches, one guided tourist activity, and a social dinner.
* A certificate of attendance will be provided.

Accommodation scholarships:
A limited number of accommodation scholarships are available for students. If you wish to apply, please indicate your interest during the registration process.

Venue:
University of Extremadura, Cáceres, Spain - a beautiful historical city and UNESCO World Heritage Site, perfect for learning and networking in a relaxed and inspiring environment.
For more information and to register, please visit: https://i3lab.unex.es/summer-school/

Co-Chairs:
Prof. Dr. Roberto Rodríguez Echeverría, University of Extremadura, Spain.
Prof. Dr. José María Conejero, University of Extremadura, Spain.

Organization:
i3Lab by Quercus Software Engineering Group and INTIA.

Sponsors:
