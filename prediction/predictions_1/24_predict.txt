### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- *The 12th International Conference on Software Defined Systems (SDS2025)* is organized with IEEE Technical Sponsorship.
- SDS2025 invites researchers, engineers, and practitioners from academia, industry, and government to explore the foundations, architectures, challenges, and breakthroughs in software-defined everything.
- It serves as a multidisciplinary platform to showcase innovations in programmability, abstraction, virtualization, and orchestration that drive software-defined solutions across domains.

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- **[SDS2025] Manuscripts should be prepared in 10-point font using the IEEE 8.5" x 11" two-column format.**
- **[SDS2025] All papers should be in PDF format and submitted electronically.**
- **[SDS2025] Papers must be original unpublished research that is not currently under review for any other conference or journal.**
- **[SDS2025] Authors of accepted papers are expected to present their work at the conference.**
- **[SDS2025] Submission category:**
  - **Full papers:** between 6 and 8 pages including all figures, tables, and references.
  - **Short papers:** between 3 and 4 pages long, including position papers and industrial demos or project proposals including PhD project proposals.
  - **Extended abstract:** High-level summary of research or project ideas (2 pages).
  - **Abstract:** Synopsis of research including ongoing projects (500 words).
