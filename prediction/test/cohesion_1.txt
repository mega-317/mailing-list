### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- Blekinge Institute of Technology is offering a position for an **ASSOCIATE SENIOR LECTURER (TENURE TRACK) in AI for SOFTWARE ENGINEERING** in Karlskrona, Sweden.
- The position is part of the strategic research area in IT and mobile communication, **ELLIIT**, which is a partnership between Linköping University, Lund University, Halmstad University, and Blekinge Institute of Technology.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- **Application Deadline**: August 1, 2025.

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- **Eligibility**: Eligible to be employed as an associate senior lecturer is a person who has completed a doctoral degree or has equivalent scientific competence. Preference should be given to those who were awarded a doctoral degree or attained equivalent competence no more than five years prior to the application deadline for employment as an associate senior lecturer. Persons who have been awarded a doctoral degree or attained equivalent competence earlier can be considered for employment if special reasons exist. Special reasons refer to leave due to illness, parental leave, or other similar circumstances.
