The IEEE International Conference on Software Maintenance and Evolution

Logistics & Sustainability

ICSME 2025 aims to be environmentally conscious and socially inclusive.
Discount codes for nearby hotels and international travel (e.g., Air New
Zealand, Qantas, Air France/KLM, etc.) will be provided upon registration.

We are also making arrangements to ensure accessibility and inclusivity for
all attendees, including those from LGBTQ+, disabled, and neurodiverse
communities.
