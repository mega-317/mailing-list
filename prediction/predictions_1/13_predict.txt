### 1. [SCOPE & IDENTITY]
- The *3rd International Conference on Intelligent Metaverse Technologies & Applications (iMETA 2025)* will take place in Dubrovnik, Croatia. 
- This will be a *Hybrid Conference* technically sponsored by IEEE Croatia section.

### 2. [TIMELINE & DATES]
- [iMETA 2025] Late Track Submission:
  - Papers due: July 30, 2025
  - Acceptance notification: August 20, 2025
  - Camera-ready paper due: August 31, 2025
- [iMETA 2025] Workshop Papers:
  - Papers due: July 30, 2025
  - Acceptance notification: August 30, 2025
  - Camera-ready paper due: September 10, 2025

### 3. [ACTIONABLE REQUIREMENTS]
- [iMETA 2025] Manuscripts must be original, not previously published, or under review by another conference or journal.
- [iMETA 2025] Submissions should adhere to the IEEE Conference Proceedings Format, using a double-column layout, 10-point font, and letter-sized paper.
- [iMETA 2025] Manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format and should not exceed 6 pages for the review process.
- [iMETA 2025] Accepted papers may extend to a maximum of 8 pages for the camera-ready version, subject to an overlength page fee.
