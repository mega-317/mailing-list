Two postdoc positions at KFUPM (https://www.kfupm.edu.sa/), Saudi Arabia

We are seeking applications for two postdoc positions in the areas of
Secure Software Development/Software Security and Artificial Intelligence
in Software Engineering. The positions are for two years, with the
possibility of extension for an additional two years based on performance
and mutual agreement.

Qualifications:
* Applicants must have, or be near the completion of, a Ph.D. in Computer
Science, Software Engineering, or a closely related field.
* Candidates currently holding postdoc positions are also encouraged to
apply.

Interested candidates are invited to send their CV or direct any inquiries
to: Prof. Mohammad Alshayeb: [log in to unmask]

We look forward to hearing from qualified candidates who are enthusiastic
about contributing to impactful research at KFUPM.

~M.
