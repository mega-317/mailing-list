CALL FOR PAPERS

PROFES 2025 – 26th International Conference on Product-Focused Software
Process Improvement

Complesso San Michele, Salerno, Italy | December 1–3, 2025

Call For Paper Website:

https://conf.researchr.org/track/profes-2025/profes-2025-research-papers

AIMS AND SCOPE

The International Conference on Product-Focused Software Process
Improvement (PROFES) is one of the leading international forums for
software engineering professionals to present and discuss innovation,
trends, experiences, and challenges in the field of software process and
product quality improvement.

The PROFES 2025 Research Track invites the submission of high-quality full
papers presenting original research, empirical studies, industrial
experiences, and case reports related to software development processes and
their impact on software product quality.

TOPICS OF INTEREST

We welcome contributions on (but not limited to) the following topics:
- Process modeling, management, assessment, and improvement
- Value- and feedback-driven software development, agile and lean methods
and processes
- Processes for (digital) software product innovation
- Software analytics and empirical research with actionable insights on
software products and processes
- Predictive models for software engineering tasks
- Infrastructure and processes for iterative or incremental software
delivery
- Continuous deployment and release planning
- Product quality models for software, systems, and services
- Project management techniques, including global and/or distributed
development
- Software engineering processes for AI-enabled systems, including MLOps
- AI and data-driven techniques for software process improvement (AI4SE)
- Methods for developing cloud-based systems and services, including
monolithic to cloud-native migration
- Software development for Cyber-Physical Systems
- Processes and methods for COTS and open-source development
- Service engineering processes and software business models


Paper submission deadline: June 13, 2025

For more details and updates, please visit the PROFES 2025 website:
https://conf.researchr.org/home/profes-2025

We look forward to your contributions to PROFES 2025!
